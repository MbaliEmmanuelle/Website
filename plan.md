# Chardous (PTY) LTD — Company Portfolio Website

## Overview
A clean, minimal, professional website for **Chardous (Pty) Ltd**, a South African distributor of medical devices and healthcare solutions. The site will have 4 pages with a working contact form.

---

## Pages

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero section** with company name, tagline ("Distributors of Medical Devices & Healthcare Solutions"), and a call-to-action button linking to Contact
- **Quick overview** of what Chardous does
- **Key highlights**: Registration No, Supplier No, SAHPRA Licence
- **Vision & Mission** summary cards

### 2. About Page
- **About Us** — full company description
- **Vision & Mission** statements
- **Core Values** displayed as icon cards (Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Customer-Centricity, Sustainability)
- **Corporate Social Responsibility** section
- **Competitive Advantages** listed out

### 3. Services Page
- **Products & Services** with categorized cards:
  - Diagnostic Equipment
  - Surgical Instruments
  - Patient Monitoring Systems
  - Hospital & Clinical Equipment
- **Compliance & Quality Assurance** section (SAHPRA, ISO 13485)
- **Client Base** — who Chardous serves (Public, Private, NGOs, Corporate Wellness)

### 4. Contact Page
- **Contact information** displayed: address (19 7th Avenue, Northmead, Benoni, 1501), phone (076 819 2303), email (info@chardouss.co.za)
- **Working contact form** (Name, Email, Subject, Message) powered by Lovable Cloud edge function that sends submissions to your email
- Clean layout with contact info on one side, form on the other

---

## Shared Components
- **Navigation bar** at the top with links to all 4 pages and the Chardous company name/logo
- **Footer** with contact details, quick links, and registration info
- **Responsive design** — works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Color scheme**: Clean white background with professional blue/dark accents fitting the medical/healthcare industry

---

## Backend (Lovable Cloud)
- One **edge function** to receive contact form submissions and process them
- Contact form messages stored in a **database table** so you can view submissions
